Yoga benefits

Yoga benefits include improved physical strength, flexibility and balance. Regular yoga proctice greatly improves mental clarity, helps with relaxation and reduces stress. In addition, yoga relieves chronic pain and boosts energy levels. Ultimately, yoga can help promote a deeper sense of connection and well-being. This allows practitioners to feel more at peace and in harmony with their bodies and the world around them.

About us


People

Certified yoga teachers and friends Alice, Bernie, Tom and Zoie have practised and taught yoga for over 10 years. They have a deep passion for helping others in finding their inner strength and balance, improving their physical and mental well-being and achieving their goals. They have created an inclusive yoga community in our town, encouraging everyone to practice with open hearts and minds.



Place

Our studio is nestled in a tranquil garden, surrounded by lush greenery and fragrant flowers. The garden's idyllic atmosphere creates an ideal setting for your yoga practice. This allows you to fully relax and unwind while moving through the most demanding poses. It also helps to create a sense of calm, enabling you to fully immerse yourself in your practice and experience the full benefits of yoga
